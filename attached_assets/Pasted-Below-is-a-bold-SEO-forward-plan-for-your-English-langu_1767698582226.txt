## 5) On-Page SEO Checklist (apply to all pages)
- Title tags: “Mobile Nail Salon in [Area] | At-Home Manicure & Pedicure Bali”
- H1: Include primary keyword + “Bali” or area.
- H2s: service variants (gel, BIAB, nail art, pedicure, callus).
- Slugs: `/mobile-nail-salon-bali`, `/gel-manicure-bali`, `/mobile-pedicure-seminyak`, `/nail-art-bali`
- Meta descriptions: 150–160 chars, include “we come to you,” area, CTA.
- Alt text: style + finish + area (e.g., “gel manicure with palm nail art in Canggu villa”).
- Internal links: from blog → service pages → booking; location hubs → specific areas.
- Schema: LocalBusiness + Service + FAQPage + Product (for bookable services with price).
- NAP consistency: name/phone/WhatsApp/hours across footer, contact, schema.
- Performance: image compression/WebP, lazy-load gallery, prefetch fonts.

## 6) Technical & UX
